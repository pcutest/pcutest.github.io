Safely Link Your Accounts

Securely “link” all of your accounts, including your 401k, so we can see your investment holdings to provide an analysis. We use bank level security and can only view your accounts (not access them). This service is provided by CashEdge by Fiserv, a leading provider of account aggregation services from more than 13,000 sources, including bank, investment, retirement and insurance accounts.

Get Connected to an Advisor

Stronghold has a national network of independent advisors. These advisors are not brokers. They provide fiduciary advice and have specific criteria including a sign off on our client BILL OF RIGHTS. These advisors don’t receive commissions for selling investments/funds and thus, you can trust they are putting your interests first.

Quick Online Enrollment

We have taken the pain out of transferring your accounts. Enrollment takes just a few minutes using our electronic docu-sign process. Your funds will be transferred to a reputable and trusted 3rd party custodian such as TD Ameritrade Institutional where your investments will remain.
