  • A HIGHER STANDARD

    A fiduciary standard is one in which the advisor is legally obligated to do
    what's in the best interest of the client at all times.
    Any and all conflicts must be disclosed or removed.
    This standard does not apply to brokers.
    It only applies to advisors who provide transparent advice,
    not sell products/funds.

A network of independence and transparency

Portfolio CheckUp has carefully and thoughtfully established a network of independent (fiduciary) advisors who are committed to transparency and the removal or disclosure of any conflicts. They are not brokers. They are held to a legal standard to do what’s in the best interests of their clients at all times and have agreed to our client “Bill of Rights:”

  • Advisors must represent the legal fiduciary standard to do what’s in the clients best interest at all times.
  • Advisors will NOT make commissions to sell/broker you investment products. They will only charge for providing advice.
  • Advisors will NOT sell you proprietary products or funds which is commonplace in many name-brand brokerage firms.
  • Advisors must have a clean regulatory record.
  • Advisor will NOT charge more than 1.25% in annual investment management fees.
